Inherits from NSObject
Declared in AGDataManager.h
AGDataManager.m

Overview

AGDataManager manages different AGStore implementations. It is basically a factory that hides the concrete instanciation of a specific AGStore implementation. The class offers simple APIs to add, remove or get access to a ‘data store’.

NOTE: Right now, there is NO automatic data sync. This is up to the user.
